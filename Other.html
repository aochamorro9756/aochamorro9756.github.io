Usability & Accessibility

I have designed my website in such a way to make it easily accessible for all of my users. All of my webpages have been evaluated by the WAVE evaluation tool to ensure that they are accessible for everyone. Secondly I have designed my website so it is easy to both use and navigate. Usability is a crucial aspect for every website to have, so it was important for me to include a cohesive and responsive layout for my users. To make my website cohesive and responsive, I used a very simplistic design and large buttons to switch between pages to make it easy to navigate.
